How to Play

The Goal

Smoke detectors run out of battery one by one. When one dies its room flashes red, the alarm chirps, and a countdown ring drains. Run to it and replace the battery before the ring empties. Miss three and the run ends.

Controls

WASDMove — arrow keys or d-pad also work
EReplace battery — when you're close (Space also)
PPause the run

Detector Types

Normal — one press to fix.
Fast — drains much quicker. Prioritize it.
Stubborn — needs two presses (shows the count).

Power-ups

Sneakers — freeze: all countdowns stop briefly.
Grape Soda — slow-mo: countdowns crawl for a while.
Chicken Bucket — speed burst: run much faster.
Heart — restores a life.

They drop on the floor and vanish if you don't grab them — the ring around each shows its timer.

Get Promoted

Keep saving batteries and you'll be promoted to a bigger property — Apartment, House, Big House, Mansion, Tower. Bigger homes mean more rooms and a higher score multiplier. Off-screen alarms show as arrows at the edge of your view.

Batteries & Perks

Your score becomes Batteries when the run ends. Spend them in the Shop on permanent perks — faster feet, steadier hands, longer reach, and extra lives — that carry across every future run.
